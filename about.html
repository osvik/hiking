About
Hiking Assistant
Purpose

An independent platform to create and share hiking routes with small and medium groups. It includes a map, satellite view, compass and weather prediction for the map region.

Benefits for hikers
  • Privacy respecting. We don't store any information about users.
  • Does not need big tech to work.
  • No ads, no tracking of any kind.
  • Works on your mobile browser, nothing to install.

About sharing your location

  • Sharing was built for hikers in groups or hiking festivals, it's not a social network!
  • It updates your location every 30 seconds.
  • Sharing only stores your latest location and your nickname. If you are offline for more than 10 minutes, it deletes it.
For website owners

The owners of this instance can create routes. To create your own routes, you can create your own instance. The software is free.

  • Share routes by sharing a URL.
  • Create a route while you hike it.
  • One simple API key to create and delete routes.
  • You can add points to a route, even if there's no Internet connection at a point.

About this app's server tech

  • Unbranded and independent app, fully open source and free. No paid tiers, no subscriptions.
  • Lightweight and easy to install. You can install it on a common PHP + SQLite server, together with WordPress, on VPSes or on many shared servers.
